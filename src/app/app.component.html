Requirements
This app requires a modern browser. If you are running Internet Explorer consider upgrading to Edge.

The app does not work well on small screens yet. Mobile support is a work in progress.
Public Lists
Anyone can open a public list as long he knows the list's name (and password).

Note that there may be multiple public lists with the same name as long as they have different passwords.
Private Lists
If you have an account you can create your own private lists.

Only you and the people you choose to share with will be able to view and edit your private lists.

You may, however, choose to share a list with everyone, even with people who don't have an account. In that case, just send them the list's link.
